Project structure


/src
  /view          <-- All your FXML files
    - main_menu.fxml
    - game_screen.fxml
  /controller    <-- Controllers that match FXML
    - MainMenuController.java
    - GameController.java
  /model         <-- Game logic (like Deck, Player, AI)
    - Card.java
    - GameEngine.java
  Main.java      <-- The app launcher
